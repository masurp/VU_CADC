Course Overview

The course consists of one lecture (starting from the 1st of November 2021) and two practical sessions. For the practical sessions, the group will be split into six groups of equal size.


Rooms and Times

  • Lectures: HG-14A00 (Monday, 9:00am)
  • Practical session 1: HG-01A32 (Tuesday, 9:00), HG-11A22 (Thursday, 13:30)
  • Practical session 2: HG-11A36 (Tuesday, 9:00 & Thursday, 13:30)
  • Practical session 3: HG-02A37 (Tuesday, 9:00 & Thursday, 13:30am)
  • Practical session 4: HG-01A32 (Tuesday, 11:00), HG-11A22 (Thursday, 15:30am)
  • Practical session 5: HG-11A36 (Tuesday, 11:00 & Thursday, 15:30am)
  • Practical session 6: HG-02A37 (Tuesday, 11:00 & Thursday, 15:30am)
